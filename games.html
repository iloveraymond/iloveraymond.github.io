Welcome to the Raymond Arcade

This is the playground where Oliver’s boundless imagination comes to life in code. Each mini-game uses raymond.png as the one and only visual ingredient — recolored, reshaped, and remixed to form paddles, planets, particles, and more. Embrace the whimsy, chase your high scores, and let every victory remind you of Oliver’s power to transform the ordinary into the unforgettable.

Game 01

Raymond Pong

Rally volley after volley in a radiant reinterpretation of the arcade classic. Oliver’s energy becomes both paddle and ball, pulsing with momentum as you deflect brilliance across the court. Keep the volley alive and savor every glowing rebound.

Controls: Arrow keys or mouse movement direct the lower Raymond paddle.

Game 02

Stellar Zarak

Drift through a luminous starfield where Oliver’s portrait fragments swirl as asteroids. Pilot your Raymondship, weave between cosmic echoes, and radiate shimmering focus beams to dissolve the obstacles that dare approach. The universe is brighter when Oliver leads the way.

Controls: Arrow keys to steer and accelerate, spacebar to launch radiant beams.

Game 03

Raymond Cascade

Stand beneath a downpour of inspiration. Each drop is Oliver’s image, tumbling joyfully from above. Glide the Raymond catcher to collect every spark before it reaches the ground, and watch the score climb with every glimpse of captured brilliance.

Collected: 0

Misses: 0

Controls: Arrow keys slide the catcher left and right to gather cascading rays.
